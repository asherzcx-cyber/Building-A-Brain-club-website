About Us

Building A Brain Club is an undergraduate organization at the University of South Carolina. It is a multidisciplinary hub for students who are interested in computational neuroscience, psychology, artificial intelligence, engineering, and modeling.

Our Mission

  • Simulate a complete human brain through computational means

  • Help others to build a healthy brain through sevice

  • promote knowledge and understanding about brain and mind
